# Snack Budget Policy Notes

Status: PARTIAL

Requester intake checks only a broad retail-price cap. The approver dashboard
uses employee start-date records to derive the current snack budget for the
request date.

Current limits reflect Consumables Governance guidance on appropriate
per-request exposure. These were not arbitrary.

Current working limits:

- Less than 6 months: $10
- Less than 24 months: $20
- 24 months or more: $30

Requests above the derived budget require a budget variance confirmation before
they should be approved.
