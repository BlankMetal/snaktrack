Hello BigCorp Family,

The Care Team is pleased to share our updated library of approved language
for use across employee-facing digital surfaces. These phrases have completed
the full Care Team review cycle and are cleared for immediate deployment.

You may select any phrase from the approved list below and append it to
operational messages on dashboards, portals, intake forms, confirmation
screens, and automated system responses.

A few reminders before you scroll:

  - Phrases are interchangeable within their designated tier.
  - Do not combine more than one approved phrase per message.
  - Care language should follow, not precede, the operational content.
  - Do not modify approved phrases. Modifications require a revision request
    submitted to the Care Team intake queue.
  - Approved Care Team language does not constitute a commitment to resolve
    the underlying issue.

We are so excited to share this with you. You matter here.

---

TIER 1 — GENERAL WARMTH

Approved for all employee-facing surfaces.

  We love you.
  We love to listen.
  You matter here.
  People matter here.
  Because we care.
  BigCorp hears you.
  Your voice shapes us.
  We see you, fully.
  You are not alone in this.
  This moment is valid.
  Our commitment to you is ongoing.
  Care is not a department. It is a posture.

---

TIER 2 — ACKNOWLEDGMENT

Approved for status updates and notice surfaces.

  We hold space for your experience.
  Your trust powers our culture.
  Growth starts with listening.
  Empathy is how we build.
  Belonging is a human right, and we agree.
  We recognize the complexity of your situation.
  Every voice matters in our care-first environment.
  Your wellbeing is central to our mission.
  BigCorp is committed to your holistic experience.
  Connection is how we grow together.
  We are proud to be part of your journey.

---

TIER 3 — PROCESS FRICTION

Approved for rejection, error, and variance surfaces. Use when the system
has been unable to fulfill a request. Append directly after the operational
message without additional explanation.

Example of correct use:

  "Snack request rejected. Snack exposure remains unowned. We love you."

  "BUDGET VARIANCE REQUIRED. Your access to care is unlimited."

Example of incorrect use:

  "We love you. Snack request rejected." [phrase must follow, not lead]

  "We love you and we hold space for your experience." [one phrase only]

Approved phrases for Tier 3 surfaces:

  Your access to care is unlimited.
  You deserve to feel heard, even now.
  Thank you for your continued engagement with BigCorp's care ecosystem.
  Your feedback is always welcome, even when it cannot be actioned at this time.
  Your snack request is important to our workplace nourishment journey.
  We are holding your concern with intention.
  This outcome does not reflect the full care you deserve.
  BigCorp sees the human behind every request.
  Your resilience is an inspiration to the team.
  Please know that this decision was made thoughtfully.

---

Questions? Contact the Care Team intake queue. Response time is typically
6 to 8 weeks. The Care Team does not have a phone number at this time.

If your concern is urgent, please consider whether it is actually urgent.
